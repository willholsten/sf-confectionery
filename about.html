About Us

S&F Confectionery is a business to business importer and distributer of mints and packaging. We are always striving to introduce innovative products to keep up with continuously changing trends. With over 100 years of experience between the senior managers, S&F Confectionery ensures to deliver unsurpassed quality and service for your company.

S&F Confectionery are now proud to be exclusively distributing the Powermints range across the UK.

Here are some of the many benefits you can expect from the range;

  • 100% pure essential oils
  • Suger free and aspartame free
  • Natural taste sensation using fruit powders
  • No artificial colouring or preservatives
  • Neutralises the ph- value in the mouth
  • prevents tooth decay with Xylitol
  • Mint flavours include the use of Stevia

Together with our huge Spencer & Fleetwood range of novelty, seasonal and designer mints, you can be assured we have mints for every occasion!

If you need something specific, we have you covered! S&F Confectionery also offer private labelling manufactured from fully certified factories. Get in touch to find out more!
